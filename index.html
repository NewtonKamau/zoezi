About

Get to know us

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginning

    Kasarani Tong-il Moo Do (TIMD) was born out of the first Kenyan Tong-il Moo Do Academy that initialized the sport in the country. Master Lwanga, previously of the Kenyan Tong-il Moo Do National team reestablished the mother club at the Kasarani aquatic stadium where it was reborn in the modern context that opened training to the greater public. The club has since grown to become one of the most successful and successful clubs in the country.

  • March 2011

    Mombasa Opens

    We participated in our first tournament in Mombasa, where we won the first prize in the national level. Our kids won the best team, best forms team.

  • December 2015

    First Tournament

    We held a first metropolitan tournament against teams within Nairobi area and we came 2nd in the overall games and we won the best club performance in techniques

  • July 2018

    Joint Trainings

    We held a joint training with the Kenya National Team and dandora team at Nairobi university grounds. We had a great time and we were able to train our kids in a very safe environment on matters hygiene, first aid and diet from proffesionals in those areas.

Welcome To Our Studio

Are you into group training? We got you, come join a class full of students of all levels.

You will gain :

  • Self-Confidence: By getting stronger and more flexible you will develop confidence in your ability to handle life's challenges
  • Focus: We will help you focus by giving clear goals and showing you how to reach these goals through hard work and consistency.
  • Self -Control: You will learn self-control in martial arts by learning how important it is to have respect, both for yourself and others. Knowing that to receive respect, you have to give it will increase self-control in the student.

Kids Karate

This class is perfect for instilling confidence, fitness, and self defense for life!

Why you kids should do martial arts:

  • Teaches Self-Defense: When children are confronted by bullies, the techniques they learn in karate gives them the ability to defend themselves.
  • Builds Confidence :In karate classes, instructors provide continual encouragement and feedback, which, in combination, builds students’ self-confidence levels as well as proper technique.
  • Hones Leadership Skills:Leadership traits depend on both self-respect and respect for others. Both of these essential forms of respect are developed during the young martial arts students’ regular training
  • Develops Character: In order for kids to advance to the next ranks, they must prove their skills. Strengthening their competence takes perseverance. Wearing upper level belts requires a demonstration of correct technique, which can only be achieved through consistent effort and dedication to the discipline.

Yoga

Yoga offers physical and mental health benefits for people of all ages. And, if you’re going through an illness, recovering from surgery or living with a chronic condition, yoga can become an integral part of your treatment and potentially hasten healing

More reasons to do yoga

  • Yoga improves strength, balance and flexibility.
  • Yoga helps with back pain relief.
  • Yoga benefits heart health.
  • Yoga relaxes you, to help you sleep better.
  • Yoga helps you manage stress.
